Product Strategy & Insights

The thinking behind IndusProtect AI — vision, research, personas, prioritization, metrics, GTM and roadmap. This section is for reviewers; the tabs below hold every deliverable in one place.

Mission

To turn insurance from a forgotten annual chore into an always-on protection partner for every IndusInd Bank customer.

Vision

A single AI-powered rail where every Indian discovers, buys, manages and claims insurance as easily as they check their bank balance.

Value proposition

One dashboard for every policy. AI that explains instead of just sells. Claims tracked live, in hours not weeks. Rewards for safe, protected behaviour.

Product roadmap
Phase 1 · Launch
Core rail — discover, compare, buy, claim, renew, wallet, rewards.
Phase 2 · AI OCR
Auto-read documents to pre-fill applications and claims.
Phase 3 · AI damage detection
Photo-based preliminary damage assessment for motor claims.
Phase 4 · Blockchain verification
Tamper-proof claim & document trail.
Phase 5 · Telematics
Usage-based pricing from driving behaviour.
Phase 6 · IoT-connected insurance
Home & vehicle sensors feeding real-time risk data.
Phase 7 · Fully autonomous insurance
End-to-end AI underwriting, pricing and settlement.
