# IndusProtect AI - Intelligent Insurance Platform

## 📋 Executive Summary

**IndusProtect AI** is a next-generation, AI-powered insurance platform that transforms the entire insurance journey from discovery to claims settlement. Built for every user—students, urban professionals, rural communities, senior citizens, and first-time users—it simplifies complex insurance processes through intelligent recommendations, fraud detection, and voice-enabled assistance.

### Project Concept
A prototype for **Product Matters 6.0** in collaboration with **IndusInd General Insurance**, demonstrating how AI can democratize insurance for Indian customers across all demographics and literacy levels.

✅ Reduce insurance buying time from **30+ days** to **2 minutes**

## 📊 How to Use - User Journey

### **Step 1: Getting Started (2 minutes)**
```
1. Open IndusProtect AI → Tap "Sign up" or "Login"
2. Enter mobile & email → Receive OTP
3. Set language preference (EN/HI/GU)
4. Choose accessibility preferences (dark mode, text size, contrast)
```

### **Step 2: Onboarding - Persona Selection**
Select your user type:
- 👨‍🎓 **Students** - Budget-conscious coverage
- 💼 **Urban Users** - Comprehensive workplace coverage
- 🌾 **Rural Users** - Vehicle & family protection
- 👴 **Senior Citizens** - Health & emergency focus
- 📱 **First-Time Users** - Guided step-by-step process

### **Step 3: AI Recommendation (5 questions, 2 minutes)**
Answer targeted questions:
1. What vehicle type? (Car, bike, commercial)
2. Annual driving distance? (< 5,000 km, 5-20K, 20K+)
3. Coverage priority? (Cost, premium coverage, specific add-ons)
4. Family size? (Solo, 2-4, 5+)
5. Budget comfort? (₹2,000-5,000, ₹5,000-10,000, ₹10,000+)

**Output**: 3-5 ranked plans with visual comparison cards

### **Step 4: Plan Comparison & Selection**
- View plans side-by-side
- Compare premium, coverage, claim ratio, garage count
- See detailed plan features
- Select and proceed to purchase

### **Step 5: Document Upload & Auto-Fill (1 minute)**
- Snap photo of RC or insurance certificate
- AI extracts vehicle details automatically
- Review & confirm extracted data
- System auto-fills policy form

### **Step 6: Policy Purchase (1 minute)**
- Review terms & conditions
- Select payment method (UPI, card, net banking)
- Confirm purchase
- Receive digital policy instantly

### **Step 7: Dashboard Home**
Post-purchase, access:
- **My Policies**: Active coverage summary
- **Claims**: File new claims, track existing ones
- **AI Chat**: Ask questions anytime
- **Renewals**: 30-day reminder before expiry
- **Rewards**: Track points and redemptions
- **Emergency**: One-tap SOS assistance

### **Step 8: File a Claim (2 minutes)**
1. Tap "New Claim" → Select incident type
2. Describe what happened (text or voice)
3. Upload evidence (photos, videos, documents)
4. AI analyzes damage & fraud risk
5. Submit → Track live updates
